I lost my father in 2007 and faced extreme difficulty both at home continuing my game. I got out of my game several years when I was focussing on my studies and helping my mother at home. My mother inspired me to go back playing table Tennis again. It look several months to gain fitness and concentrate on the game. After trying very hard for few years I could make it to the Nationals again in 2017. Though I cloud qualify for men's singles at the nationals but lost in the semi finals at Ranchi. After coming home started working on my fitness again and with my coach. I went back to my coach this time with a stronger regime since I am unable to cross the Semi finals. In the Nationals in 2019 I was stopped in the Semi Finals again. By now I understood that I have to work differently to reach the top. I went back to my coach again. I was by then focussing on my job to support my mother. I appeared for interview and test for TCS. It was a moment of great joy to be able to get a job in TCS as a Web Design/ Developer. though I was posted in bangalore I kept my practise in the office after work. Then Covid strike. I came home in kolkata. I was worried that my fitness and my game will be affected. As the lockdown got relaxed, I went back to my practics and started meeting my coach again. This time had to work double hard to get back my game. I got selected for the nationals again for this year. I knew that I have to win this time so focussed on every match. I played with lot of confidence. kept winning one by one. After i reached Semi finals this time. I was determined to cross the line Finally. I made it and was among the top 2 players in the country.
